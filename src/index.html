What does Hedge™ do?

Multiple backups

Easily create copies for yourself, post-production, the director and others. Always make sure your files are safely stored.

File verification

Every copy made by Hedge™ is made from the source material, and cross-referenced with other copies to ensure a perfect bit-for-bit copy.

Safety in numbers

Every card added to Hedge™ is guaranteed to have been copied to all the drives you specified. That way you won’t erase footage that hasn’t been ingested yet.
