Features

Multiple backups

Create backups for yourself, post-production, the director, your mum. Hedge imports files while verifying others, optimizing your bandwidth and saving precious hours.

File verification

Say adiós to corruption. Unlike with the Finder, every file imported by Hedge is cross-referenced with other copies to ensure it’s bit-for-bit perfect.

Extendable workflows

Want to go further? Let Hedge autolaunch your AppleScripts, to process your footage to your heart’s content. Sort, transcode, rename, tag, do whatever you'd like to do.
